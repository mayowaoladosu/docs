## Unified Billing

Nubis provides a transparent, usage-based billing system. You only pay for what you use, with detailed tracking for every resource.

### Payment Methods
Manage your payment sources in **Billing > Payment Methods**.
- **Credit/Debit Cards**: Securely add and manage cards through Stripe.
- **Regional Payments**: Support for Paystack (Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa).
- **Primary Method**: Designate a primary card for automatic monthly payments.

### Invoices
All past and current invoices are available in the **Billing > Invoices** section.
- **PDF Download**: Download official invoices for your accounting records.
- **Billing History**: View a complete history of all charges and payments.

### Usage and Quotas
Track your resource consumption in real-time.
- **Usage Metrics**: See how much you're spending on Compute, Storage, and Networking.
- **Project Breakdown**: View costs per project.
- **Quotas**: Manage your resource limits (e.g., maximum number of Instances). You can request quota increases directly through the Console.

## Billing Alerts
Set up automated alerts to notify you when your monthly spending reaches a specific threshold, helping you avoid unexpected costs.
