## Production-Grade Databases

Nubis offers managed database services that take the complexity out of storage management. Focus on your application while we handle the infrastructure, backups, and scaling.

### Supported Databases

We currently support the most popular open-source databases:
- **PostgreSQL**: The world's most advanced open-source relational database.
- **MySQL**: A reliable and widely-used relational database.
- **Redis**: Low-latency, in-memory data store for caching and real-time data.

### Features

#### Automated Backups
We take daily snapshots of your database to ensure your data is always safe. You can restore your database to any point in time within our retention period.

#### High Availability
Our managed databases can be deployed with standby nodes that automatically take over if the primary node fails, ensuring your application stays online.

#### Monitoring and Insights
Get detailed performance metrics, including query latency, connection counts, and resource utilization, to help you optimize your database performance.

#### Security and Encryption
- **Encryption at Rest**: All database data is encrypted on disk.
- **Encryption in Transit**: Connections to your database are secured via SSL/TLS.
- **VPC Integration**: Keep your database traffic entirely within your private network.

### Scaling your Database

Need more power? You can scale your database's CPU, Memory, and Storage with a single click in the console, ensuring you're always ready for traffic spikes.
