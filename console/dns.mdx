## DNS and domain lifecycle

Nubis combines DNS management with domain workflows so teams can move from search to delegation without juggling multiple tools.

## Core zone management

- Create and manage **DNS zones** inside a project.
- Add, update, and delete common record types such as **A**, **AAAA**, **CNAME**, **MX**, and **TXT**.
- Re-check registrar and delegation status when a domain is mid-migration or awaiting propagation.

## Domain operations

Nubis also supports project-scoped domain workflows:

- **Search** for available domains before purchase.
- **Checkout and verification** for new registrations.
- **Renewal and order tracking** for domains already managed through Nubis.
- **Transfer-related verification** where applicable.

## Registrar controls

After a domain is registered or connected, Nubis can help manage lifecycle actions such as:

- Reviewing registrar information.
- Updating nameserver settings.
- Toggling domain lock or privacy settings.
- Retrieving auth codes when needed.
- Managing auto-renew and contact details.

## Recommended workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create or connect the zone">
    Add the DNS zone to the project that owns the workload and confirm delegation status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish the required records">
    Point the domain at your load balancer, floating IP, or other target resources.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track orders and renewals">
    If Nubis is also handling the domain purchase, keep an eye on order state, payment, and renewal timing.
  </Step>
</Steps>
