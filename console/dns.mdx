## Global DNS Infrastructure

Nubis provides a high-performance, globally distributed Anycast DNS service that ensures your users are always connected to the nearest available resource.

### Managing DNS Zones
A DNS Zone represents a domain name (e.g., `example.com`).
1. Navigate to **Networking > DNS**.
2. Click **"Add Domain"**.
3. Update your domain's nameservers to point to Nubis:
   - `ns1.usenubis.com`
   - `ns2.usenubis.com`
   - `ns3.usenubis.com`

### Supported Records
We support all major DNS record types:
- **A & AAAA**: Map hostnames to IPv4 and IPv6 addresses.
- **CNAME**: Alias one hostname to another.
- **MX**: Specify mail servers for your domain.
- **TXT**: Store text-based information (SPF, DKIM, etc.).
- **SRV**: Define service locations.
