## Identity & Access Management (IAM)

Nubis IAM allows you to securely manage access to your cloud resources at scale.

### User Management
Invite your team members to your organization and manage their lifecycle from a central dashboard.
- **Invitations**: Send email invites with specific initial roles.
- **Account Status**: Monitor active, pending, and deactivated users.
- **Activity Logs**: View [Audit Logs](/console/security) for each user.

### Roles and Permissions
Nubis uses Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) with pre-defined and custom roles.
- **Organization Roles**: High-level roles that control account-wide settings (e.g., Billing Admin).
- **Project Roles**: Resource-level roles that control what users can do within a specific project (e.g., Compute Manager, Networking Viewer).

### Service Accounts
Create identities for automated services, CI/CD pipelines, and background workers.
- **API Keys**: Manage secure tokens for programmatic access.
- **Scopes**: Limit service account access to specific regions or resource types.

## SSO Integration
Enterprise customers can integrate their existing identity providers (Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace) using SAML or OIDC for seamless, centralized authentication.
