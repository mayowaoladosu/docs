## Custom Images

Nubis allows you to create and manage your own custom operating system images, enabling you to deploy pre-configured environments instantly.

### Creating a Custom Image
You can create a custom image from any existing [Instance](/console/instances) or [Snapshot](/console/backups).
1. Navigate to your Instance.
2. Select **"Capture Image"**.
3. Choose a name and tags for your image.

Once created, your image will appear in the **Compute > Images > Custom** tab.

### Marketplace Images
We provide a wide range of pre-configured images for popular software stacks:
- **Web Servers**: Nginx, Apache, LiteSpeed.
- **App Frameworks**: Node.js, Python/Django, Docker.
- **One-Click Apps**: WordPress, Ghost, Strapi.

## Private Registry
Your custom images are stored in your organization's private registry, ensuring they are only accessible to your team members.

## Image Lifecycle Management
- **Deprecation**: Mark older images as deprecated to prevent new deployments while keeping them for reference.
- **Sharing**: Share images across different [Projects](/console/projects) within your organization.
