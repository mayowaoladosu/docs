## Platform Status

Stay informed about the health of the Nubis platform across all global regions.

### Live Status
The **Observability > Status** page shows the real-time status of all core Nubis services:
- **API Gateway**: Performance and availability of the control plane.
- **Provisioning System**: Success rates for resource creation and management.
- **Managed Services**: Health of Compute, Database, and Networking clusters.

### Active Incidents
When an issue occurs, our team provides detailed incident reports directly in the console:
- **Timelines**: Real-time updates as we work toward a resolution.
- **Impact Analysis**: Which regions and services are affected.
- **Post-Mortems**: Public reports detailing the root cause and long-term fixes after an incident is resolved.

## Subscription
Subscribe to status updates via Email or Webhooks to get immediate notifications about any platform-wide events.
