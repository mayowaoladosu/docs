## Unified Log Management

Nubis Centralized Logs aggregate logs from all your resources—Instances, Containers, Databases, and Load Balancers—into a single, searchable interface.

### Log Explorer
The **Observability > Logs** page provides a powerful exploration interface powered by Loki.
- **Real-time Streaming**: Watch your logs as they arrive.
- **Rich Filtering**: Filter by resource ID, log level (INFO, WARN, ERROR), or custom tags.
- **Time Range Selection**: Quickly jump to specific historical time windows.

### Infrastructure Logs
We automatically ingest logs for:
- **System Logs**: `/var/log/syslog` and `journald` from your instances via the [Nubis Agent](/backend/services).
- **Access Logs**: HTTP request logs from your Load Balancers and App Platform services.
- **Database Logs**: Slow query logs and system events from managed databases.

## Long-term Retention
Define custom retention policies for your logs in the **Governance > Settings** page. You can store logs for days, months, or even years to meet compliance requirements.
