## Real-time Observability

Nubis provides a comprehensive monitoring solution that gives you visibility into every layer of your cloud infrastructure.

### Standard Infrastructure Metrics
We automatically collect performance data for all your resources:
- **CPU & Memory**: Track utilization and identify bottlenecks.
- **Disk I/O**: Monitor storage performance and latency.
- **Network Bandwidth**: Track inbound and outbound traffic.
- **Database Performance**: Monitor query latency and connections.

### Custom Dashboards
Create your own visualizations in the **Observability > Dashboards** section.
1. **Add Widget**: Choose from line charts, bar charts, heatmaps, and more.
2. **Select Metric**: Pick any metric from our wide catalog.
3. **Define Scope**: Filter by project, region, or specific resource tags.

## Alerting
Never miss a performance issue. Configure automated alerts to notify you via Email, Slack, or PagerDuty when metrics cross specific thresholds.
- **Static Thresholds**: Alert when a value exceeds a limit (e.g., CPU > 90%).
- **Anomaly Detection**: Alert when metrics deviate from their normal historical patterns.
