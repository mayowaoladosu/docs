## Organizations

An Organization is the highest-level container in Nubis, representing your business or team. It manages billing, high-level security policies, and team membership.

### Creating an Organization
When you sign up, a default organization is created for you. You can create additional organizations from the **Organization Switcher** in the top navigation bar.

### Team Management
In the **Organization > Members** section, you can:
- **Invite Members**: Add team members by their email address.
- **Assign Roles**: Assign roles (Admin, Member, Billing) to control access levels.
- **Manage Invites**: Cancel pending invitations or resend them.

## Organization Settings
Change your organization's name, logo, and primary contact information in the **Organization > Settings** page.
