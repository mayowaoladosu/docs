## Getting Help

We're here to help you succeed with Nubis. We offer multiple channels for support based on your needs.

### Documentation
Our comprehensive [Documentation](/) is the first place to look for answers. It covers everything from getting started to advanced API integrations.

### Support Tickets
For technical issues or account inquiries, you can open a support ticket in the **Support** section of the Console.
1. Click **"New Ticket"**.
2. Select a category (Billing, Technical, Account).
3. Provide a clear description and any relevant resource IDs or screenshots.

### Community & Resources
- **Discord Community**: Join our vibrant community of developers and engineering teams.
- **Service Status**: Check [status.usenubis.com](https://status.usenubis.com) for real-time information on any ongoing incidents or maintenance.
- **Blog**: Read our latest platform updates and engineering deep-dives at [usenubis.com/blog](https://usenubis.com/blog).

## Priority Support
Enterprise organizations have access to priority support with dedicated Slack channels and faster response times. Contact [sales@usenubis.com](mailto:sales@usenubis.com) to learn more.
