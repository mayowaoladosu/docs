## Recommended first path

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up the workspace">
    Start in [Get started](/get-started) to define the organization, team, project model, and billing posture.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the service">
    Go to [Nubis Compute](/services/nubis-compute), [Nubis Storage](/services/nubis-storage), or [Nubis Databases](/services/nubis-databases) depending on the workload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the focused task pages">
    Use the smaller, task-oriented guides under each service instead of broad catch-all pages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use support and status intentionally">
    Check [Need help?](/need-help) and [status.usenubis.com](https://status.usenubis.com) when you need faster troubleshooting.
  </Step>
</Steps>
