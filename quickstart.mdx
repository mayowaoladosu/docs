## Launch your first Instance

Nubis makes it incredibly easy to deploy virtual machines. Follow these steps to get started.

### 1. Sign Up
Head over to [console.usenubis.com](https://console.usenubis.com) and create your account. You'll get immediate access to the dashboard.

### 2. Create a Project
Resources in Nubis are organized into Projects. Create your first project to keep your infrastructure organized.

### 3. Deploy an Instance
Click the **"Create Instance"** button in your project dashboard.
- **Select a Region**: Choose a location closest to your users.
- **Select an Image**: Choose from Ubuntu, Debian, or our custom high-performance images.
- **Select a Size**: Choose a plan that fits your resource needs.

### 4. Connect to your Instance
Once your instance is live, you can connect via the web console or SSH:

```bash
ssh root@your-instance-ip
```
